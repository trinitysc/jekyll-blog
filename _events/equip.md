Registrations for the Equip Conference are now closed, but Equip evening sessions are free and open to anyone. They start at 7.30pm, taking place on 6-10 January at Kaiapoi High School. Vaughan Roberts (St Ebbe's, Oxford) and Murray Capill (Reformed Theological College, Melbourne) will be the keynote speakers. Find out more at [https://equipconference.org.nz](https://equipconference.org.nz)
