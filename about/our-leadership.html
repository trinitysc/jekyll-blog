At Trinity, the Ministry Team is never just the paid staff and pastors. We believe that Jesus has given every Christian the gifts and opportunities to serve (minister) to their fellow Christians and to the community around them. We see this happening at Trinity week-in and week-out as people express love to one another, pray for one-another, serve, provide, train and teach.

Governance

Trinity is governed by a Parish Council, comprised of the Senior Minister and ordained assistant clergy, Churchwardens, and elected members and the Treasurer. The Wardens, along with the Senior Minister, are the legal officers of the church.

  • Churchwardens: John McKie & Tim Wilson
