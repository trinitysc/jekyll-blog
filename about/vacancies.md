## Come Join our Team!

We're looking for an Assistant Minister / Ministry Director to join the senior staff team at Trinity South Christchurch.

If you're someone who loves Jesus, loves his church, and finds your greatest joy in seeing others grow and flourish in ministry, we'd love to hear from you. This is a role for someone who prayerfully leads by developing others - equipping, releasing, and walking alongside people.

Trinity is a growing church in South Christchurch. We're captivated by Jesus, committed to the gospel, and compelled to make disciples. It's a joy to be part of what God is doing in South Christchurch as he grows a community of people increasingly passionate about delighting in God and reaching our city for Christ. We'd love a godly, gifted team-player to join our church family – we're keen to shape the exact nature of the role around the right person's gifts.

A full position description is available. We welcome expressions of interest from ordained and unordained candidates, and we'd warmly encourage you to get in touch with James directly if you'd like to find out more.
