Come and discover the joy, peace, and hope found in the greatest gift of all, Jesus Christ.

Whether you're a regular churchgoer, an irregular churchgoer, a visitor, or just looking for a change of pace from All I want for Christmas is You, you'll be most welcome at any of our services. Bring the kids to our pop-up Nativity on the 14th, or come for mulled wine, Christmas cake, candlelight and carols at 10:45pm on Christmas Eve.

Find us at 200 Colombo St, Sydenham. Have questions? We'd be happy to help.

We are located in Sydenham, just south of the Christchurch CBD.

Best access is via Colombo Street from the north or south, Tennyson St from the east; or Strickland St from the west.

There is limited car parking on site. The best streets to park on are Roxburgh St, Boon St, Southey St.
