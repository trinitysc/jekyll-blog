How to find us

We are located in Sydenham, just south of the Christchurch CBD.

Best access is via Colombo Street from the north or south, Tennyson St from the east; or Strickland St from the west.

There is limited car parking on site. The best streets to park on are Roxburgh St, Boon St, Southey St.

Find us on Google Maps.

Public Transport

Metro bus route 1 stops just outside the church. Travel time from the CBD is 10-15 minutes. Buses leave approximately every 15 minutes. When you see KFC on the left, it's nearly time to disembark. (Which is perhaps also good advice in general.)

For other routes, see the Metro route map.
