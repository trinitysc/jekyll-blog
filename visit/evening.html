On 2 February, Trinity starts doing evening church! Each Sunday we will meet from 6pm for an intimate and informal evening service.

We will be exploring the claim that Jesus is God’s King as we work through Mark’s Gospel. Afterwards we will share a meal together, where you are welcome to ask questions.

Come and check out our warm and relaxed community!

You'll find us at 200 Colombo Street.
