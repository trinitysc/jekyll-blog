New to 2025, each Sunday we will meet from 6pm for Evening Church; an intimate and informal evening service. Come a little earlier if you fancy some chips and chocs before the service.

We will be exploring the claim that Jesus is God’s King as we work through Mark’s Gospel. Afterwards we will share a (free) meal together, where you are welcome to ask questions.

Come and check out our warm and relaxed community!

You'll find us at 200 Colombo St, Sydenham. Questions about the service? Check out our FAQs.
