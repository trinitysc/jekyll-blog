Investigating Christianity

Trinity runs a course called Introducing God – a chance for you to explore who God is and how you can know him. It’s a relaxed and friendly environment where no question is off limits, you can wrestle with big questions or sit back and listen to ideas from other people. The course runs periodically, if you’d like more information or to register for the next one please be in touch.

Thinking of making Trinity your home?

If you are thinking of making Trinity your home we’d love to invite you to our Welcome Lunch. This is a chance for you to meet the Trinity Team and for us to explain more about what we’re passionate about at Trinity. It’s a great chance to meet others who are new or considering making Trinity home. There will be a chance to ask any questions you may have. To register or for more info please be in touch.
